### Recording Software Options

**OBS Studio** (Recommended - Free):
- Download: https://obsproject.com/
- Pros: Professional features, highly customizable, free
- Cons: Steeper learning curve
- Settings: 1920x1080, 30fps, 5000-8000 kbps

**Loom** (Simple - Free tier available):
- Website: https://www.loom.com/
- Pros: Dead simple, browser-based, auto-uploads
- Cons: Limited editing, watermark on free tier
- Best for: Quick recordings, minimal editing

**ScreenFlow** (Mac - $169):
- Website: https://www.telestream.net/screenflow/
- Pros: Recording + editing in one, high quality
- Cons: Mac only, paid
- Best for: Mac users wanting all-in-one solution

### Editing Software Options

**DaVinci Resolve** (Recommended - Free):
- Download: https://www.blackmagicdesign.com/products/davinciresolve
- Pros: Professional-grade, free version powerful
- Cons: Resource-intensive, learning curve

**iMovie** (Mac - Free):
- Built-in on Mac
- Pros: Simple, intuitive, fast
- Cons: Mac only, limited features

**Adobe Premiere Pro** (Paid - $22.99/mo):
- Industry standard
- Pros: Most powerful, extensive features
