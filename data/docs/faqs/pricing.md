# Pricing FAQ

## What are the pricing tiers?

OrchestratAI offers three pricing tiers:

1. **Free Tier**: Up to 1,000 API calls per month
2. **Pro Tier**: $49/month for 50,000 API calls
3. **Enterprise**: Custom pricing for unlimited usage

## Do you offer annual discounts?

Yes! Annual subscriptions receive a 20% discount.

## Can I upgrade or downgrade at any time?

Absolutely. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

## What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and wire transfers for Enterprise customers.

## Is there a free trial?

Yes, all new users get a 14-day free trial of the Pro tier.
