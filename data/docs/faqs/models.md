# Models FAQ

## What LLM models are supported?
GPT-4, GPT-4 Turbo, GPT-3.5, Claude 3 Opus, Claude 3 Sonnet, Claude 3 Haiku.

## Can I use my own model?
Enterprise plans support custom model endpoints.

## How do I choose the right model?
GPT-4 for complex tasks, GPT-3.5/Haiku for speed, Opus for creative work.

## Are embeddings models customizable?
Yes, you can configure embedding models in your account settings.

## Do you support open-source models?
Not directly, but Enterprise plans can integrate custom endpoints.
