# Limits FAQ

## What are the rate limits?
Free: 10 req/min, Pro: 100 req/min, Enterprise: Custom.

## Is there a token limit per request?
Depends on the model. GPT-4 supports up to 8K tokens.

## How many documents can I upload?
Free: 100 documents, Pro: 10,000 documents, Enterprise: Unlimited.

## What's the maximum file size?
Individual files can be up to 50MB.

## Can I increase my limits?
Yes, upgrade your plan or contact sales for custom limits.
