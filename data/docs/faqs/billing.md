# Billing FAQ

## How is usage calculated?

Usage is calculated based on API calls and tokens processed.

## When am I billed?

Billing occurs on the first of each month for the previous month's usage.

## Can I set spending limits?

Yes, you can configure spending caps in your account settings.

## What happens if I exceed my plan limits?

Requests will return 429 errors until you upgrade or wait for the next billing cycle.

## Do you offer refunds?

We offer pro-rated refunds for annual plans within 30 days.

## Can I get usage reports?

Detailed usage reports are available in your dashboard and via API.
