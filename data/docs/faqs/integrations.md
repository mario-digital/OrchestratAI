# Integrations FAQ

## What integrations are available?
OrchestratAI integrates with Slack, Discord, Microsoft Teams, Zapier, and Make.

## How do I set up a Slack integration?
Navigate to Settings > Integrations > Slack and follow the OAuth flow.

## Can I integrate with my CRM?
Yes, we support Salesforce, HubSpot, and Pipedrive integrations.

## Is there a Zapier integration?
Yes, search for "OrchestratAI" in the Zapier app directory.

## Do you have a REST API?
Yes, our full REST API is documented at api.orchestratai.com/docs.
