# Acceptable Use Policy

## Permitted Uses

- Business automation
- Customer support
- Research and development
- Educational purposes
- Content generation

## Prohibited Uses

- Illegal activities
- Harassment or abuse
- Spam or phishing
- Malware distribution
- Unauthorized access attempts
- Impersonation
- Copyright infringement

## Content Restrictions

Do not generate:
- Illegal content
- Hate speech
- Personal data without consent
- Medical/legal advice
- Misleading information

## Enforcement

Violations may result in:
- Warning
- Service suspension
- Account termination
- Legal action

## Reporting Abuse

Report violations to: abuse@orchestratai.com
