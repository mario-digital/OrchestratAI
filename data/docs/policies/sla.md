# Service Level Agreement (SLA)

## Uptime Guarantee

### Pro Tier
- 99.5% monthly uptime guarantee
- Service credits for downtime exceeding threshold

### Enterprise Tier
- 99.9% monthly uptime guarantee
- Priority support and incident response
- Dedicated account manager

## Response Times

### Support Tickets
- Critical (P1): 1 hour response
- High (P2): 4 hour response
- Medium (P3): 24 hour response
- Low (P4): 48 hour response

### API Performance
- Average latency: < 500ms (95th percentile)
- Maximum timeout: 30 seconds

## Maintenance Windows

- Scheduled maintenance: Tuesday 2-4 AM UTC
- 72 hour advance notice for planned downtime
- Emergency maintenance may occur without notice

## Service Credits

Downtime exceeding SLA thresholds earns service credits:
- 99.0-99.5%: 10% credit
- 95.0-99.0%: 25% credit
- < 95.0%: 50% credit

Credits applied to next month's bill.

## Exclusions

SLA does not cover downtime caused by:
- Customer configuration errors
- Third-party service failures
- DDoS attacks
- Force majeure events

Monthly reports available in dashboard
